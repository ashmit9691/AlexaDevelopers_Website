    About Us

    Welcome to Alexa Developers SRM, a thriving, student-run technical organization at SRMIST that brings technology and community together. We focus on voice-controlled technologies, application development, and UI/UX design via hands-on events and workshops. Whether you're a newcomer or a seasoned professional, our events provide ongoing learning and skill development.

    Together we build a community, where creativity knows no bounds and collaboration is key.
